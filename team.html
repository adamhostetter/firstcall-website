About FirstCall

One organization. One team. 24 branches.

FirstCall Group is a multi-region commercial mechanical service platform built by industry operators. We came up through the trades — installing, servicing, and managing the systems that keep commercial buildings running.

We started FirstCall to do one thing well: help leading regional mechanical service businesses keep doing what they do best, while plugging into a national support platform that takes the back-office burden off their shoulders. HR, finance, IT, marketing, and operations expertise — pooled, professionalized, and shared.

Our team has decades of combined experience in commercial HVAC, building controls, plumbing, and project work. We support local leadership, invest in technicians, and let the business owners we partner with focus on customers and growth.
