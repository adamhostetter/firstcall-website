What we build

The kind of projects we take on.

Mid-sized commercial retrofit work where mechanical and controls expertise matters more than the lowest bid.

Building retrofits

Office, warehouse, light industrial, and institutional buildings — mechanical system overhauls that bring older buildings up to current efficiency and code.

Tenant fit-outs

New tenant build-outs and reconfigurations — VAV layouts, dedicated cooling, controls zoning, and code-compliant ventilation.

System expansions

Adding capacity to existing chiller plants, boiler rooms, or air-side distribution — sizing, integration, and commissioning of the new work.

Process & specialty cooling

Server rooms, lab spaces, clean rooms, and process cooling loops — precision cooling and redundancy where it matters.

Energy upgrade projects

VFDs, ECM motors, controls optimization, and equipment right-sizing — utility-rebate-eligible work with measurable payback.

Controls upgrades

BAS conversions, sequence-of-operation rewrites, and graphics rebuilds — bringing your controls platform back into service.

Our process

How a project goes from idea to commissioning.

1

Walk & scope

We come look at the building, talk to your team, and understand what the actual problem is — not just what's been quoted to you.

2

Design & estimate

Engineering review, equipment selection, and a transparent estimate with line-item pricing — not a black-box lump sum.

3

Build

Self-performed install with our own technicians and pipefitters. Tight coordination with your operations team on access and downtime.

4

Commission & turn over

Functional testing, controls verification, as-built documentation, and operator training. The team who installed it stays available to service it.
